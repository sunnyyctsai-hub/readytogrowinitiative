WELCOME TO READY TO GROW

<

At Ready to Grow Initiative, we believe every child should have the opportunity to reach their full potential. We are a student-led community movement that provides free workshops and caregiver resources designed to create a nurturing developmental environment for children aged 1-6 and their caretakers. By working together with venues connected to the underserved, we bring accessible, engaging, and research-based support to children and caretakers!

Starting Ready to Grow, we have donated 150+ educational kits to Orange County Rescue Mission, grown to 20+ international board members, started 2 school chapters, and partnered with 5 organizations that support children and families. Through workshops, outreach, and caregiver education, we hope to continue making developmental support more accessible for every child.

WORKSHOPS

Each session focuses on a key developmental area through hands-on activities for the kids, while offering caregivers tools and strategies to continue supporting development outside of the workshops!

CURRICULUM

Our workshop curriculums are tailored to the developmental stage of each child, including activities scientifically shown to assist development. These curriculums undergo multiple stages of review and refining to assess the plausibility, degree of helpfulness, executability, and interactivity.

More Info

CAREGIVER EDUCATION

Ready to Grow offers clear, practical guidance that helps caregivers understand how young children learn, behave, and develop. Caregivers gain confidence and useful techniques they can use every day to support growth at home and in their community.
